HOW WE CAME TO BE

Downtown Iron was created with personal progress at the forefront of our minds. Working out at commercial gyms, we found judgement and unhelpful criticism was the ruling attitude. After enduring years of unpleasant interactions with other gym-goers, we decided that we wanted to create a gym with an atmosphere of support and accountability. Downtown Iron is a gym that cultivates constructive criticism and positive reinforcement. Here, we seek to improve ourselves and each other.
